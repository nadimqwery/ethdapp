Kon'tract Platform Services

Definition


Kon'tract token is a decentralized application (Dapp) token made for the purpose of selling in an ICO to fund the Kon'tract platfrom's development and also for fueling operations of the Kon'tract platfrom.

Kon'tract token is deployed on the Ethereum blockchain.


Why buying Kon'tract coins?


Users willing to use the Kon'tract platfrom have to buy Kon'tract coins since it's the only cryptocurrency used on the platfrom.
